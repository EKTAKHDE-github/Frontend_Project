Kim Taehyung

Kim Taehyung is a singer, dancer, songwriter, composer, director, producer, actor, host and a model. He made his professional debut in 2013 as a member of the global pop band BTS under the stage name "V". He is the eldest of three children, with a younger brother and sister. V first aspired to be a professional singer in elementary school,and means of pursuing the career. V initially became a trainee for Big Hit Entertainment after passing an audition in Daegu.V released his first full solo track outside of BTS, "Scenery", on January 30, 2019 through the group's SoundCloud page. The ballad was written and composed by V.he also photographed the cover artwork and produced by Big Hit producer Docskim, with additional contributions from two other Big Hit producers, Pdogg and Hiss Noise. It broke SoundCloud records for reaching 100 million streams from 20 million in over fourteen days, the shortest time for the platform. He has won awards in areas of singing, acting and choreography. And in 2022 won the Conscientious Taxpayer award from National Tax Service.
